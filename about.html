About us.

KS 18, established in 1995, is a trusted and well-known provider of authentic BMW and Mercedes Benz spare parts in Phnom Penh. Since shifting from car sales to spare parts in 2003, we have proudly served customers across the entire country, with clients from neighboring countries also relying on us for our high-quality, imported parts. We stand by the authenticity of our products, ensuring that each item is sourced from trusted international suppliers.
